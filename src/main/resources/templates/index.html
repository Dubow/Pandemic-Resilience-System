How It Works

1. Government Sets Rules

Officials create critical items and define purchase limits such as maximum quantity per day, week, or month.

2. Merchants Add Stock

Registered merchants create stores and update available stock for essential goods.

3. Public Users Purchase

Citizens select available items, purchase within government rules, and manage their PRS profile.
